Guide's Instructions for Teaching with ISLmate

As a guide, your role is crucial in helping students effectively learn Indian Sign Language using the ISLmate platform. Follow these steps to ensure a smooth and engaging learning experience.

Step 1: Introduction to ISLmate

  • Website Overview
    • Start by giving the students a walkthrough of the ISLmate website.
    • Explain the structure of the platform, including sections like Lessons, Assessments, and Resources.
  • Navigation Guidance
    • Demonstrate how to access videos, use interactive elements, and navigate between lessons and assessments.
    • Highlight any accessibility features, such as video playback controls and captions.
  • Purpose and Goals
    • Clearly outline the objective of the session: to learn and practice Indian Sign Language in an interactive way.

Step 2: Teaching the Signs

  • Demonstrate and Explain
    • Use the videos and images on the platform as teaching aids.
    • Pause videos where necessary and explain the hand movements and expressions step-by-step.
  • Hands-On Assistance
    • Guide students in practicing the signs. Physically assist them if needed (with their consent) to correct their hand positions and movements.
  • Encourage Repetition
    • Encourage students to repeat the signs multiple times to build confidence and accuracy.
  • Use Examples
    • Provide real-life examples of when and how these signs are used to make the learning more relatable.

Step 3: Conducting Assessments

  • Prepare Students
    • Before starting an assessment, review the relevant signs and ensure students are comfortable.
  • Facilitate Practice
    • During the assessments, encourage students to practice the signs displayed on the flashcards.
    • Motivate them to try their best and not worry about mistakes.
  • Track Progress
    • At the end of Assessment 1 and Assessment 3, ask students to report how many signs they got correct.
    • Record their responses to monitor progress and provide feedback.

Step 4: Providing Support

  • Encourage Questions
    • Create an open and supportive environment where students feel comfortable asking questions.
  • Offer Individual Attention
    • Pay attention to students who may be struggling and offer additional guidance.
  • Positive Reinforcement
    • Celebrate their efforts and improvements to keep them motivated.

Step 5: Concluding the Session

  • Recap and Reinforce
    • Summarize the key signs learned during the session.
    • Highlight any improvements or areas for continued practice.
  • Assign Practice
    • Encourage students to practice regularly using the platform and to revisit lessons as needed.
  • Feedback
    • Ask students for feedback on the session to identify areas for improvement in future classes.

Tips for Effective Learning for Students

  • Practice daily to build muscle memory.
  • Use a mirror to check your hand movements.
  • Stay patient and enjoy the learning process!

Thank you for helping students embark on this rewarding journey with ISLmate!
